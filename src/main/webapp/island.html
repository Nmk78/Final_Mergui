Paw Daw Mu Pagoda

After a long time of being isolated from the outside world, the Myeik Archipelago’s accessibility now is unrestricted and more open for tourism. Our Myeik Archipelago tours & day trips bring you to the isolated archipelago which is home to more than 800 beautiful islands which are created in different kinds of shapes. Located in the Andaman Sea off the coast of southern Myanmar, the Myeik Archipelago (or Mergui) is considered as one of the planet’s most unspoiled destinations. The islands and surrounding seas look viable thanks to the diversity of flora & fauna system and stunning underwater scenes and marine life.

Thein Daw Gyi

Thein Daw Gyi Pagoda is another important religious site in Myeik, offering a peaceful atmosphere and a stunning view of the surroundings. It is a perfect spot for meditation and quiet reflection.
